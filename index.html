Mustering Muses


In Ancient Greece, the Muses were invoked by artists to serve as sources of inspiration for their work. Indeed, inspiration is what fuels our actions and provides a sense of purpose. TED as a society believes passionately in the power of an individual and her ideas to change the world. TEDxIITRoorkee2017: Mustering Muses aims to unleash our untapped potential and seeks to summon the very source of inspiration from within each of us.
